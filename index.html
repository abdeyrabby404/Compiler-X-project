How Converter Works?

How to use the Converter: Step-by-Step

1

Choose Operation

Start by selecting the transformation or analysis you need — for example, Left Recursion Elimination to remove recursive patterns, Left Factoring to simplify grammar choices, or First & Follow Set Calculation to aid parsing table construction.

2

Enter Grammar

Type or paste your grammar rules into the input field, making sure they follow the required format for accurate processing.

3

Generate & Use

Click "Generate" to instantly receive the transformed grammar or calculated sets. Review the results, then copy and apply them directly to your Compiler Design assignments, projects, or practice exercises.
